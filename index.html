Fill in the gaps

Chuleta inteligente de examen

  • Present Perfect: have/has + past participle.
  • Past Perfect: had + past participle.
  • Passive: be + past participle.
  • Third Conditional: if + past perfect, would have + participle.
  • Reported Speech: cambia tiempos, pronombres y referencias temporales.
  • Relative Clauses: who, which, that, where, whose.
Truco PAU: antes de responder, identifica si la frase habla de pasado, presente, hipótesis, voz pasiva o estilo indirecto.

Writing Checker

Escribe un opinion essay y la web analiza longitud, conectores, estructura, vocabulario útil y claridad. No es una IA real, pero parece una herramienta avanzada.

Task

Write an opinion essay: “Should students use artificial intelligence to study?”



Plantilla para un essay de 10

Introduction: Present the topic and your opinion.
First argument: Explain one clear reason.
Second argument: Add another reason or example.
Counterargument: Mention the opposite view.
Conclusion: Summarise your opinion.

Connectors top: Firstly, Moreover, However, In my opinion, For instance, As a result, To sum up.
